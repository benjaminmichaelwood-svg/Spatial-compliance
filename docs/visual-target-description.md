The visual target is a PointStudio compliance-to-design thickness heatmap showing:
- Black background
- Isometric 3D view of open pit mine
- Smooth gradient painted on the production surface
- Colour scale (right side legend): Underdig (white 10m, red 1m, yellow 0.5m) through zero to Overdig (green -0.5m, cyan -1m, blue -20m, black)
- Green = near design (0 to -0.5m overdig), Yellow = slight underdig, Red = significant underdig (1-10m), Blue = significant overdig
- The gradient follows terrain contours smoothly across benches, ramps, and batters
- Purple/dark areas where no design surface exists (boundary edges)
- Pink annotation circle and line highlighting a specific area
